How Allocations Work

  • Funds can only be transferred to Approved shelters.
  • Allocations are deducted from the global donation pool (if tracked) and added to the shelter's specific available balance.
  • Once authorized, the funds will reflect immediately on the shelter administrator's dashboard.

Transfers are processed instantly via our payment gateway integrations.
